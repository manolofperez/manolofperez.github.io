Talks

Biogeography and evolution of bony-tongue fishes at Universitätsklinikum Jena

Here I presented the current results I am gathering for the Osteoglossiformes order, as part of my post-doc. I also presented an example of using Deep Learning for demographic model assessment.

Lectures

Spatial Methods in Phylogeography and Population Genetics - UFSCar 2019

A one week course that I gave to Genetics and Evolution Master's and PhD students in 2019. Classes were focused in spatial analyses carried in R. I talked about Species Distribution Modeling, Isolation by Distance, Isolation by Environment and Population Structure. All scripts, slides and tutorials can be found here (classroom code bpd12k):

  • https://classroom.google.com/c/MzY4NTk0NTMwMjha.
